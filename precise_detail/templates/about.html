About Precise Detail

Precise Detail offers professional cleaning services with over 30 years experience cleaning private residences. My name is Rochelle, owner of Precise Detail. In addition to my 30 plus years in residential housecleaning, I also have experience cleaning hospitals, offices, hotels, and nursing homes as well as doing move-outs and detailing out new construction homes for Parade of Homes. I have narrowed my focus to private residences in order to bring you the quality and attention to detail you expect from a professional.

I work as an individual in order to keep costs down for my clients. This also allows us to develop a relationship and a bond of trust. I have had multiple clients from 8 years to 20 years.
